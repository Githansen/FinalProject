Not recycling can have devastating consequences



  • It takes 500 years for average sized plastic water bottles to fully decompose

  • Over 100,000 marine animals die every year from plastic entanglement and ingestion

  • Glass bottles take 4,000 years to decompose

Recycling is the first step towards reducing emissions



  • Increasing the US' recycling rate from 34.5% to 75% would be the equivalent of removing 50 million fossil-fueled cars from circulation

  • Producing one new can requires more energy than making 20 cans from recycled material

  • Recycling in the UK saves approximately 18 million tons CO2 annually

Everyone has to recycle better



  • In Oslo, citizens produce, on average, 89 kg food waste per capita, which of 39 kg (45 %) is correctly source separated in green bags. For plastic waste the average amount per capita is 17 kg, which of 5 kg (31 %) were correctly source separated in blue bags.

  • In 2018, 57 % of the household waste was sent to energy recovery. By using state of the art technology, the waste combustions assure that the energy production is environmentally friendly.

  • The energy recovery plant produces enough electricity to power all the schools in Oslo, and the heat produced is distributed into the city’s district heating system.
